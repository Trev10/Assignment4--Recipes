Best Homemade Lemonade Ever

This lemonade recipe makes a very refreshing drink!

  1. Prep Time: 10 mins
  2. Cook Time: 5 mins
  3. Total Time: 15 mins
  4. Serings: 10
  5. Yield: 10 8-ounce servings

Ingredients

Water: 1 cup

Sugar: 1 3/4 cup white sugar

Lemon: 9 medium lemons or more as needed

Ice: as needed


Conversion

=

Directions

Juice your lemons:

Start by cutting your lemons in half crosswise and squeeze out the lemon juice by hand or with a juicer.

Making the syrup:

In a medium saucepan, combine the sugar and water. Cook over medium heat and slowly stir until the sugar dissolves.

Mix them up and enjoy:

Add the lemon juice to the sugar syrup and stir to combine. Then pour the syrup into a large pitcher add ice as needed and enjoy the home made lemonade!


How to make sure your lemonade is fresh

Store it in the fridge without ice and only add ice when you put the lemonade in your cup to ensure the lemonade will not be diluted and make sure it didn't sit in the fridge for more than 5-7 days.


Recipe Tips

  • You can store premade syrup for up to a month to skip a step.
  • 9 medium lemons should give you about 1 and a half cup of lemon juice.
